ABOUT US

Black & White is a Indian based clothing Brand Black & White is… having a million perspectives Black & White is… to grow wild and free Black & White is… Picking up a fetish and calling it style” Known for his flamboyant batting style on-field, Virat Kohli has raised quite a few eyebrows with his effortless sense of style off the field as well. Be it his stunning haircuts or his non-conformist attitude, his style has always been a clear reflection of his personality. Spotted most frequently in smart casuals, Virat teams up funky shirts with slim-fit denims and perfectly set hair, giving him a clean and bold look. Thereby, his classic-rugged look never goes out of style. Black & White, his first fashion brand, caters to the open-minded and progressive men. It’s a brand which gives people a chance to express themselves, indulge in freedom and set an example, much like the swashbuckling superstar himself. Knowing that the outfits are of extraordinary fit and style, Black & White seeks an evolved world for itself and the like-minded. An eclectic collection of apparel that challenges established fashion trends and norms, the range is inspired by Virat’s distinctive sense of style and enormous youth appeal. In a nutshell, Black & White is an extension of Virat’s bold and dynamic personality which is expressed in each piece of clothing. So this season the only way to go right fashionably, is to go Black & White!
